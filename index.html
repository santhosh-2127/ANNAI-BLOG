Location

Annai College of Engineering and Technology is located in Kovilacheri, Kumbakonam, Tamil Nadu. It is situated on Anakudi Road, near Kovilacheri, about 10 km from Kumbakonam Town on the Kumbakonam-Chennai Highway.

About Us

Welcome to the Department of Information Technology and Computer Science Engineering. Our mission is to empower students with cutting-edge knowledge in software development, data science, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and more.

With experienced faculty, modern labs, and industry-driven curriculum, we ensure our students are equipped for both academia and the tech industry. We emphasize innovation, practical skills, and teamwork to build the next generation of tech leaders.

Join us to explore the dynamic world of technology and shape your future with creativity and code!

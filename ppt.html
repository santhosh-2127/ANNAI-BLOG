PAPER PRESENTATION: SHARE YOUR IDEAS WITH IMPACT

Team Size: Maximum 3 participants per team

Guidelines:

  • Participants can choose their own topics. It is recommended to focus on a single specific theme. For any clarifications, please contact the event coordinator.
  • The abstract should not exceed 250 words, and the paper should be limited to a maximum of 12 pages.
  • Bring your PowerPoint presentation on a pen drive for the event.
  • Soft copies of the paper must be submitted in .docx or .pdf format. Email your paper and presentation to ithodacet@gmail.com by 28.04.2025.
  • The submission email should include:
    • Title / Theme of the paper
    • Names of the authors
    • Contact numbers of all authors
    • Email IDs of all authors
  • Presentations must be submitted in .pptx format.
  • Each team will be allotted 10 minutes for the presentation, followed by a Q&A session.
  • The paper must follow the IEEE Format.
  • The paper should include: Index, List of Figures, List of Tables, Abstract, Introduction, Subject Description, Conclusion, Result, and References.
  • After submission, a panel of judges will review the papers. If shortlisted, you will be notified. The judges' decision is final and binding.

SHORT FILM CONTEST: CREATING CINEMATIC MASTERPIECES

Duration: 2 hrs (10:00 am to 12:00 pm)

Plot:

  • Filmmakers will have just two hours to create an original short film from scratch, working under intense time pressure.
  • The film must adhere to a specific theme, while maintaining high-quality storytelling, cinematography, and sound design.
  • All participants must use the provided elements, such as footage, soundtracks, or dialogue, while adding their own creative flair.
  • Collaboration outside the team is strictly prohibited – only the film crew’s creativity can guide them to victory.
